About the project

Curse of The Jungle is my first video game. It was made by a group of 5 students as an assignment to teach us 2D game dev in GameMaker. It's my first serious experience with programming. Though it may not seem like much, I've put a lot of effort into it, and spent nearly double the hours in game engine than my peers, which allowed us to deliver well-received product at the end. It was also my first team managing GitHub repository for a group of people.
