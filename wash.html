About the project

W.A.S.H. was a semester-long student project made by 5 students. Our task was to advertise “Union of Waterboards” in the Netherlands. It consisted of a lot of research and prototyping. This project was my first real experience with Unity, and taught me a lot about programming.

My Contributions

Underwater Movement

Heavy, submarine-like movement. Controlled with mouse or/and keyboard, integrated with HUD, stabilizes camera, can be quickly edited through Inspector.

Radar

Minimap that shows pickable objects and the distance to them.

Object Pickup

Slowly moves object towards the submarine, then picks them up.
